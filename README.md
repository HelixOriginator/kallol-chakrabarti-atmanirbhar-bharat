## 🌱 Project Nano Banana — Open Defensive Publication Framework

> *Transforming "Patent Poverty" into "Patent Democracy"*

**The Problem:** Brilliant ideas from students, farmers, and grassroots inventors across India are lost — not for lack of innovation, but lack of funds for patent filing.

**The Solution:** The Nano Banana framework enables any citizen to establish legally recognised **Prior Art** through secure, timestamped digital disclosures — **at zero cost**.

**Key Features:**
- ✅ Zero cost — accessible to every Indian citizen
- ✅ Legally recognised timestamped disclosures
- ✅ Searchable national archive of indigenous citizen-inventions
- ✅ Protects India's intellectual borders from corporate appropriation

**Proof of Concept:** Shortlisted for **National IP Awards 2025** (CGPDTM, Government of India) on the strength of 1 provisional patent and 25 registered copyrights — *without a granted patent*, confirming substantive originality.

**National Proposal:** A **National Open Defensive Publication Platform as Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)** — where any citizen timestamps their invention for free.
